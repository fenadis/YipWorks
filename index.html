Our Mission

At YipWorks, we believe businesses shouldn't have to choose between overpriced agencies and unreliable freelancers. That's why we built a different kind of development team — one that helps both sides win. We connect talented computer science students and recent grads with businesses that need affordable, reliable software solutions. Our developers gain real-world experience working on meaningful projects, and our clients get high-quality results without the overhead, delays, or bloated pricing of traditional firms. Whether you need a custom dashboard, internal automation, or a full web app — we deliver clean, practical software with a fast turnaround and a personal touch.

A Message From the Founder

I started YipWorks after graduating from the University of Southern California. Like many new grads, I saw firsthand how tough it was for smart, capable students to break into the tech industry. Some of the best developers I knew were overlooked, not because they weren't qualified, but because they didn't fit neatly into a hiring pipeline. YipWorks began as a way to change that — to give those peers a real chance to build and grow through real client work. What started as a side project to help friends has grown into something bigger: a way to support emerging developers while delivering results that businesses can truly rely on.

- Alex Yip, Founder
